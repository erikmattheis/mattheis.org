Key Features

  • Budget Management: Utilizes budget management functionalities to allocate resources effectively for advertising campaigns.
  • Campaign Configuration: Configures Google Ads campaigns based on the specific requirements of GreatClips franchise owners.
  • Ad Group Customization: Creates ad groups with tailored settings to optimize the targeting and delivery of advertisements.
  • Keyword Integration: Incorporates relevant keywords to enhance the visibility of ads in response to user search queries.
  • Proximity Targeting: Implements proximity targeting to ensure that ads are displayed based on the user's geographical location.
  • Error Handling: Includes error handling mechanisms to identify and address potential issues during the campaign creation process.

Technologies and Tools

  • Express.js: API for interacting with Google Ads services, facilitating the creation and management of advertising campaigns.
  • Node.js: JavaScript runtime for executing server-side code.
  • node-adwords Library: Node.js client library for interacting with the Google Ads API, facilitating the creation and management of advertising campaigns.
  • xml2js Library: Converts XML data to JSON format, aiding in processing and handling data retrieved from the Google Ads API.
  • async Library: Manages asynchronous operations in a waterfall pattern, ensuring a sequential flow of tasks during the campaign creation process.
  • BudgetHelper Module: Custom module for handling budget-related functionalities.
  • CampaignHelper Module: Custom module for managing campaign-related functionalities.

This application simplifies the process of setting up and managing Google Ads campaigns for GreatClips franchise owners, enhancing the visibility and reach of their franchises in response to user searches.
